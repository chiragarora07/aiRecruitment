The Problem

Campus recruitment today is broken

The same painful process, repeated every semester — and it never improves.

Manual & Time-Consuming

Teams spend weeks reading CVs and coordinating offline — time that could go into actual work.

Gut-Feel Decisions

Shortlisting is inconsistent and biased. There's no structured rubric — just vibes and WhatsApp debates.

Zero Candidate Experience

Results arrive days late via WhatsApp forwards. Candidates wait, wonder, and lose trust in the process.

Features

Everything recruitment needs, nothing it doesn't

Built for the way campus orgs actually work — from the first application to the final email.

AI CV Screening

Parses every uploaded CV and scores skill-fit, experience alignment, and project relevance against the role.

Personalised Interview Questions

AI generates 5–8 unique, CV-aware questions per candidate. No two candidates see the same set.

Structured Scorecard

Interviewers submit per-criteria scores and written remarks. No free-form chaos — every score is on record.

Weighted Score Matrix

CV, AI, and PI scores combined via admin-set weightages. Adjust them anytime — rankings auto-recompute.

Automated Result Emails

One click sends personalised acceptance, waitlist, or thank-you emails to every candidate simultaneously.

Role-Based Access Control

Admin, Applicant, and Interviewer roles enforced at the server level — not just hidden on the frontend.

How It Works

One linear pipeline. Five locked stages.

Every stage gates the next. No skipping, no shortcuts — just a clean, auditable process.

1

Org Setup

Admin creates org, drive, and sets stage weightages

2

Open Drive

Candidates discover and apply before the deadline

3

AI Screening

CV scored and personalised questions generated for every applicant

4

PI Round

Interviewers view AI scores and submit structured scorecards

5

Results

Final scores aggregated; result emails dispatched automatically

Roles

Built for every person in the room

Three distinct roles. One shared goal: a fair, fast recruitment.

Admin

Full platform control

  • Create and manage organisations
  • Open recruitment drives with custom weightages
  • Assign interviewers to candidates
  • View the full score matrix and make selections
  • Dispatch result emails in one action

Applicant

Your shot at a fair process

  • Discover orgs and browse active drives
  • Submit application with CV upload
  • Complete the timed AI interview round
  • Track status across all stages in real time
  • Receive automated, personalised result emails

Interviewer

Walk in prepared, decide with data

  • View assigned candidates' full profiles and CVs
  • See AI-generated questions and candidate responses
  • Review AI score before the personal interview
  • Submit a structured scorecard with per-criteria marks
  • Scores lock on submission — no second-guessing
